PUMPKIN PIE:

Ingredients:


  • For the crust:
    • All Purpose Flour
    • salt
    • butter
    • cold water
  • For the filling:
    • cooked pumpkin
    • evaporated milk
    • eggs
    • sugar/brown sugar
    • cinnamon
    • ginger
    • nutmeg

  • Steps: (Overview)

    1. Mix the flour and salt. Cut in the shortening and add water.
    2. Shake the dough into the ball.
    3. Roll it out on a lightly floured surface.
    4. Cut the rolled dough and fit it into the pie pan.
    5. Beat the filling ingredients together, then pour it into the prepared crust.
    6. Bake in a preheated oven until a knife comes out clean.

And Done!
