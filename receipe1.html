1. PUMPKIN PIE:

Ingredients:
  • For the crust:
    • All Purpose Flour
    • salt
    • butter
    • cold water
  • For the filling:
    • cooked pumpkin
    • evaporated milk
    • eggs
    • sugar/brown sugar
    • cinnamon
    • ginger
    • nutmeg
  • Steps: (Overview)
    • Mix the flour and salt. Cut in the shortening and add water.
    • Shake the dough into the ball.
    • Roll it out on a lightly floured surface.
    • Cut the rolled dough and fit it into the pie pan.
    • Beat the filling ingredients together, then pour it into the prepared crust.
    • Bake in a preheated oven until a knife comes out clean.

And Done!
