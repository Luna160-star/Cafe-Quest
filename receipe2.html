2. MOCHA FUDGE BROWNIES

Ingredients:
  • INGREDIENTS:
    • 1. 1/2 cup butter
    • 2. 1 cup sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 tbsp instant coffee
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup cocoa power
    • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
Steps:
  • Preheat oven to 175 degree celsius, grease a square baking tray.
  • Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, coffee and vanilla
  • Add cocoa, flour, and salt. Mix until thick.
  • Pour into the tray and then bake for 25 minutes.
  • Let cool and then cut them in squares of you like.
  • If, you want, use a top with a drizzle of a chocolate ganache. Enjoy!!
