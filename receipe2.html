MOCHA FUDGE BROWNIES

Ingredients:

  • INGREDIENTS:
    • 1. 1/2 cup butter
    • 2. 1 cup sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 tbsp instant coffee
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup cocoa power
    • 3/4 cup all purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degree celsius, grease a square baking tray.
  2. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, coffee and vanilla
  3. Add cocoa, flour, and salt. Mix until thick.
  4. Pour into the tray and then bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Let cool and then cut them in squares of you like.
  6. If, you want, use a top with a drizzle of a chocolate ganache. Enjoy!!

And Done!
