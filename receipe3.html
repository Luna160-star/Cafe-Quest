3. LEMON DRIZZLE LOAF

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Zest + one lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter
Steps: (Overview):
  • Mix all the ingredients like flour, baking powder, sugar, eggs and butter into one bowl.
  • Pour the batter into the mold and bake for 30-35 mins.
  • Pour lemon glaze (sugar +juice) on top of it.
