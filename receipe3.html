PUMPKIN SPICE LATTE

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk (pref oat milk)
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin puree
  • whipped cream (opt)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar or maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp pumpkin spice or cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp pumpkin spice or cinnamon

Steps: (Overview):

  1. In a saucepan, whisk milk, pumkpin puree, sugar and spice together on a low flame hear.
  2. Keep whisking till the milk is hot and frothy. (Not boil)
  3. Add freshly brewed coffee and stir gently
  4. Pour into a mug, top with whipped cream and dust cinnamon on top
  5. If youd like drizzle some caramel on top of it.

And Done!
