CARAMEL PUDDING

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Steps:(Overview)

  1. Melt sugar in a pan and add water. This is for the caramel.
  2. Mix milk, eggs, and sugar.
  3. Now pour caramel and the prepareed ingredients into the mold.
  4. Steam for 20 mins or bake in water bath.

And Done!
