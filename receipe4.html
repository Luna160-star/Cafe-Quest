CARAMEL PUDDING

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
Steps:(Overview)
  • Melt sugar in a pan and add water. This is for the caramel.
  • Mix milk, eggs, and sugar.
  • Now pour caramel and the prepareed ingredients into the mold.
  • Steam for 20 mins or bake in water bath.
