PUMPKIN PANCAKES

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
Steps:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and cinnamon.
  • 2. In another bowl, whisk pumpkin puree, milk, egg, and melted butter
    • 3. Combine all the prepared ingredients and mix until it is smooth.
  • 4. Heat a pan with a little butter and pour 1/4 cup butter at a time.
  • 5. Flip when bubbles form on top and edges look dry.
  • 6.Stack them all and drizzle maple syrup or whipped cream.
