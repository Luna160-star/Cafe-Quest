PUMPKIN PANCAKES

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. 1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and cinnamon.
  2. 2. In another bowl, whisk pumpkin puree, milk, egg, and melted butter
    3. 3. Combine all the prepared ingredients and mix until it is smooth.
  3. 4. Heat a pan with a little butter and pour 1/4 cup butter at a time.
  4. 5. Flip when bubbles form on top and edges look dry.
  5. 6.Stack them all and drizzle maple syrup or whipped cream.

And Done!
