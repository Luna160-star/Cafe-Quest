SWEET POTATO FRIES WITH MAPLE GLAZE

Ingredients:
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt + pepper
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • Pinch of cinnamon or chili flakes (optional)
Steps:
  • 1. Peel and cut the sweet potato into fries.
  • 2. Spread on a baking tray with space between each fry.
  • 3. Bake at 400 degree F for about 25-20 minutes, and making sure to flip them in between.
  • 4. Once crisp and golden, drizzle maple syrup and sprinkle cinnamon.
