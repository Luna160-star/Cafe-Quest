Vanilla Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Steps: (Overview)

  1. Preheat Waffle Maker.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add milk, eggs, butter, and vanilla, whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour Batter into the waffle maker and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve with maple syrup or chocolate drizzle.

And Done!
