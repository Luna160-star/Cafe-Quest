7. Vanilla Waffles

Ingredients:
  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
Steps: (Overview)
  • Preheat Waffle Maker.
  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  • Add milk, eggs, butter, and vanilla, whisk until smooth.
  • Pour Batter into the waffle maker and cook until golden brown.
  • Serve with maple syrup or chocolate drizzle.
