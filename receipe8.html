BLUEBERRY MUFFIN

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
Steps: (Overview)
  • Firstly, preheat the oven to about 375 degree F and line a muffin tray with cups
  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar and baking powder.
  • In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, butter and vanilla.
  • Mix both the ingredients prepared.
  • Fold in the blueberries gently.
  • Pour the prepared batter into cups and bake for 20 mins.
  • Let it cool and done!
